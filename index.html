About The Campaign

Dedicated to Justice Across America

Founded by Collin McDonald, this campaign was born from a deep commitment to protecting ordinary Americans from the rapidly growing threat of financial fraud, online scams, and organized criminal networks. Our mission: no victim should face these crimes alone.

Working in close cooperation with the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3), our team investigates reported cases, coordinates victim advocacy, and pursues every available legal avenue to recover stolen funds and hold perpetrators accountable.
